What payment methods are available?

The means of payment can be:
— Bank Transfer (In Argentine pesos). — MercadoPago (In Argentine pesos). — Payoneer (In US dollar or euro). — Binance (USDT, Bitcoin or Etherium).

Do you have a payment term?

Yes!. The payment is 50% in advance and the other 50% when the work is finished.

What do I receive once the process is completed?

Once the process is completed, you receive the complete project, already online and functional. You also have the option of contracting a Support and Maintenance system for it.

Once the project is delivered, do I have to pay anything else?

Having a web page always has a fixed payment, either monthly or annually, depending on the owner, these expenses are the host of the page and the domain.
What is the host? It is the site where the page is stored.
What is domain? The domain is the unique name that identifies your page on the internet.

If I want to make changes to my page, who do I turn to?

In addition to the development service, we have the support and maintenance service, in which we fully take care of the changes and modifications that you want to make to the page, as well as if any inconvenience arises in it.
